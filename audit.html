What the audit covers

We look at every layer
of your system.

The Revenue Recovery Audit is not a generic checklist. It is a structured analysis of your specific digital infrastructure — designed to identify the highest-priority failure points and produce a clear recovery roadmap.

Website & SEO — how your site performs, how it ranks, what's missing structurally, and whether it captures intent when visitors arrive
Inbound handling — how fast leads are responded to, what happens after first contact, where prospects are going cold
Social media & content — whether your content is producing enquiries or just activity, and what the conversion gap is
Automation & tools — what you currently have, what's missing, and where human delay is costing you revenue
Recovery roadmap — a prioritised list of what to fix first, second, and third — with estimated revenue impact
